Gesture Control

Control your presentations with hand gestures

Gesture Guide

Navigation

  • Move hand right → Next slide
  • Move hand left → Previous slide

Zoom Control

  • Pinch up → Zoom in
  • Pinch down → Zoom out

End Presentation

  • Hold three fingers up for 1 second
  • Press ESC key to exit manually
